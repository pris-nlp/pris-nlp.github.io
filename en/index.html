Research Direction

What we are doing

Multi-round Dialogue

Natural language understading, out-of-distribution intent detection, multi-round dialogue management, natural language generation and related applications.

Automatic Question Answering

Retrieval-based question answering(RBQA), knowledge-base question answering(KBQA)、community-based question answering(CQA) and related applications.

Intelligent Summarization

Dialogue summarization, Online learning based summarization, Consistency of summarization, Zero/few shot summarization, Domain Transfer for summarization and related applications.
