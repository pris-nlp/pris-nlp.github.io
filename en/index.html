Research Direction

What we are doing

MoE

Train larger-scale models within limited computational resources. Reduce costs and improve inference speed.

Inference and Code

Dedicated to enhancing the capabilities of models in complex reasoning tasks, including coding, mathematics, logical reasoning, and other tasks.

Multimodal

Face Perception Generalist, Large Vision-Language Model.

Model Editing and Parameter Efficiency

Large Language Model Model Editing, Efficient Fine-tuning of Large Language Models.

Alignment and Security

Promoting Alignment of Language Models with Human Preferences through Instructional Fine-tuning, Reward Learning from Human Feedback, and other methods.

Agent

Reasoning and Planning,Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM),Tools Learning
